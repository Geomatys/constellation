About Constellation-sdi

Constellation-SDI makes it possible to easily create a complete Spatial Data Infrastructure, from cataloguing geographic resources to operating a platform of sensors that feeds back information in real time.


Existing services

  • CS-W 2.0.2 (Conforme INSPIRE)
  • WMS 1.1.1 & 1.3.0 (Conforme INSPIRE)
  • WMTS 1.0
  • WFS 2.0 (Conforme INSPIRE)
  • WCS 1.1 (2.0 en cours de développement)
  • SOS 2.0
  • WPS 1.0 (2.0 en cours de développement)
